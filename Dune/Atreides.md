# Atreides

You win through information.

Your early game is about **preserving Arrakeen and monetizing information without fully commoditizing it**. Your income comes from the planet, and your movement-3 advantage matters. Defend your city, bid selectively on high-impact cards, use spice-deck information to choose favorable harvest races, and sell battle information in a way that keeps everyone dependent on you rather than fully informed.

Midgame, fight only when prescience or card tracking lets you compress losses.

Late game, Atreides wins by appearing disciplined until the round where discipline becomes a sudden multi-stronghold jump.

Basic plan:

- Gather information.
- Buy selectively.
- Sell information without giving away the whole table.
- Form targeted deals.
- Fight only when your information converts into a real edge.
- Punish overconfident opponents.

Atreides often wants an ally who supplies money, battle compression, or a reliable finishing threat.
