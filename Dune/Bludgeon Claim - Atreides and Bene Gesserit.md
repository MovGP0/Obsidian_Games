# Bludgeon Claim - Atreides and Bene Gesserit

Claim:

> The correct alliance is Atreides and Bene Gesserit.

Atreides plus Bene Gesserit is a very strong package because information plus battle control compresses uncertainty. Atreides helps identify safer fights and better purchases. Bene Gesserit can distort combat with Voice and prediction pressure.

But "the correct alliance" is too absolute. The better rule is:

> Ally to fix your bottleneck.

Atreides may need money more than Voice. Bene Gesserit may need board presence more than information. Emperor, Guild, Fremen, and Harkonnen can all be correct partners depending on the current board.

Assessment:

> High synergy, overstated absolutism. Strong pair; not always the correct pair.
