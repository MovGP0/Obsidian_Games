# Faction Tracks and Alliances

Dune: Imperium has four faction tracks:

- [[Dune/Emperor|Emperor]]
- [[Dune/Spacing Guild|Spacing Guild]]
- [[Dune/Bene Gesserit|Bene Gesserit]]
- [[Dune/Fremen|Fremen]]

Reaching 2 influence with a faction gives a victory point. Reaching 4 influence gives a bonus, and the first player to 4 takes that faction's alliance token. If another player passes them, the alliance moves. This is one of the main scoring lanes in [[Dune/Strategy Overview|Strategy Overview]].

## Strategy

Faction tracks matter because they combine:

- Direct points.
- Alliance pressure.
- Access requirements for some spaces.
- [[Dune/Deck Building and Card Quality|Card]] synergies.
- Opponent denial.

## Practical Heuristic

Do not spread influence randomly. Pick tracks because they support your leader, your cards, your [[Dune/Dune Imperium Base Game|base-game]] route, your expansion module, or a realistic alliance race.

Sometimes the right move is not to win an alliance, but to threaten it enough that the current holder must spend actions defending it.
