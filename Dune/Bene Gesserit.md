# Bene Gesserit

The Bene Gesserit faction track is one of the four main influence tracks in Dune: Imperium.

## Strategic Identity

Bene Gesserit access often supports card quality, intrigue pressure, and efficient influence routes. The value depends heavily on which cards are available and whether you can turn influence into points quickly.

## When To Prioritize

Prioritize Bene Gesserit when:

- Your [[Dune/Deck Building and Card Quality|cards]] already point there.
- You can gain a faction point without disrupting your main route.
- You can threaten or steal an alliance.
- You want intrigue or card effects that support your plan.
