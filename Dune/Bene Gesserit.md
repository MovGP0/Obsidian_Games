# Bene Gesserit

You win through manipulation, prediction, battle control, and timing.

The strongest beginner rule is:

> Do not let your low revival rate drag you into normal attrition.

In the basic game, your free shadow-shipping goes into **Polar Sink**, which gives central reach but does not equal the full advanced-game advisor and coexistence toolkit many experienced players associate with the faction.

Your board game is quieter in basic, but your battle leverage is still elite because Voice can erase key card lines.

Early game: build a hand and remain hard to read.

Midgame: intervene when a win must be denied or when your opponent is exposed to Voice-based punishment.

Late game: weaponize prediction pressure even when the prediction itself is not live. The threat changes behavior.

Bludgeon's phrasing that Bene Gesserit combines strong combat ability with an ability to avoid combat captures the spirit, but in pure basic the "avoid combat" side is more about timing and deterrence than advanced coexistence tricks.

Basic plan:

- Avoid normal attrition.
- Build a useful hand.
- Use Voice to distort battle incentives.
- Intervene when a win must be denied.
- Keep prediction pressure alive.
- Use alliances to turn timing control into a finishing threat.
