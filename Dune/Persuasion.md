# Persuasion

Persuasion is reveal-turn buying power. It buys Imperium row cards and, at high thresholds, [[Dune/The Spice Must Flow|The Spice Must Flow]].

## Strategic Role

Persuasion is strongest when your [[Dune/Deck Building and Card Quality|deck]] is built to reveal well. [[Dune/High Council|High Council]], draw effects, and focused card buys make persuasion more reliable.

It is weaker when your cards need to be spent on agent turns or when the game is ending before new cards matter.
