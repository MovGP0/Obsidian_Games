# Dune Imperium Base Game

The base game is the cleanest version of the system: [[Dune/Agents and Worker Placement|worker placement]], [[Dune/Deck Building and Card Quality|deck-building]], [[Dune/Faction Tracks and Alliances|faction tracks]], [[Dune/Combat and Conflicts|conflicts]], [[Dune/Intrigue Cards|intrigue cards]], and [[Dune/The Spice Must Flow|The Spice Must Flow]].

## Strategic Emphasis

- Early [[Dune/Swordmaster|Swordmaster]] is often a major [[Dune/Opening Priorities|tempo goal]].
- [[Dune/High Council|High Council]] supports repeated high-[[Dune/Persuasion|persuasion]] reveals.
- Faction access is valuable because influence creates points and alliance races.
- Conflicts matter most when they award points or scarce conversion resources.
- Deck quality matters because the starting deck has limited faction access.

## Main Routes to Points

- Reach 2 influence on faction tracks.
- Take or steal alliances.
- Win important conflicts.
- Buy [[Dune/The Spice Must Flow|The Spice Must Flow]].
- Score intrigue cards.
