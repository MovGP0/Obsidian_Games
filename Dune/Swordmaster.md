# Swordmaster

Swordmaster gives you a third [[Dune/Agents and Worker Placement|agent]]. It is one of the most important tempo upgrades because every later round can contain one more agent turn.

## Strategic Role

Swordmaster is strongest when the extra agent immediately creates better actions: faction access, resource conversion, combat deployment, card draw, or a point route.

It is weaker when buying it consumes [[Dune/Solari|solari]] that could score directly, when your hand lacks useful icons, or when the game has already shifted into [[Dune/Game Phase Identification|late-game]] conversion.

## Timing

Early Swordmaster is a common goal in [[Dune/Dune Imperium Base Game|base Dune: Imperium]]. [[Dune/Rise of Ix|Rise of Ix]] can enable fast Swordmaster lines through shipping and solari tempo.

In [[Dune/Uprising|Uprising]], Swordmaster cost differs by timing: the first player pays more than later players, so the race has a different texture.
