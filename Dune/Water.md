# Water

Water is an access resource. It is valuable because it unlocks spaces, cards, spice, and troop deployment lines.

## Strategic Role

Water is often strongest early when it opens otherwise unavailable spaces. It is also a useful pivot when the obvious [[Dune/Solari|solari]] or shipping line is blocked.

Water is weaker when you collect it without a target. Always ask what space or scoring route the water unlocks.
