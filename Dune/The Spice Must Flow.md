# The Spice Must Flow

The Spice Must Flow is the main repeatable persuasion-based scoring route in base Dune: Imperium.

## Strategic Role

The card matters because it turns a strong reveal into a victory point. A player who can repeatedly buy it can score without needing conflict wins or faction alliances every round.

The route usually wants:

- [[Dune/High Council|High Council]].
- Reliable [[Dune/Persuasion|persuasion]].
- Card draw or deck quality.
- Enough time before the [[Dune/Game Phase Identification|late game]] ends.

## Watch For

If an opponent can reach the buy threshold, denial may matter more than your own marginal engine action. Count this in the [[Dune/Late Game Checklist|late game]].
