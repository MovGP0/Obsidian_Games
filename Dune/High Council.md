# High Council

High Council improves reveal turns by adding persuasion. It is a scoring engine when your deck can reliably convert that extra persuasion into cards, especially [[Dune/The Spice Must Flow|The Spice Must Flow]].

## Strategic Role

High Council is not automatically correct. It is strongest when:

- Your [[Dune/Deck Building and Card Quality|deck]] already has good reveal value.
- You can repeatedly reach high-persuasion thresholds.
- You are pursuing a [[Dune/Persuasion|persuasion]] scoring route.
- The game is not ending before the seat pays back.

It is weaker when your cards mainly want agent turns, combat, or faction access instead of reveal power.
