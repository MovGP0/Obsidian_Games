# Spice

Spice is the flexible high-value resource. It becomes powerful when it has a clear conversion path.

## Main Uses

- Pay for [[Dune/Highliner|Highliner]]-style combat pressure.
- Support expensive card and board effects.
- Enable [[Dune/The Spice Must Flow|The Spice Must Flow]] routes in the base economy.
- Interact with expansion systems such as [[Dune/Rise of Ix|tech]].

## Strategic Role

Spice can look like a lead while doing nothing. It is strong when it becomes points, combat leverage, tech, or denial. It is weak when it sits unused while other players score.
