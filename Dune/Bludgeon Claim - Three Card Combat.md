# Bludgeon Claim - Three Card Combat

Claim:

> With merely three cards you can win almost any leader to leader duel.

The idea is useful: weapon, defense, and battle-control information can make a fight extremely favorable.

The literal claim is too broad. Traitors, cheap hero, lasgun and shield risk, incomplete information, and board-state consequences still create failure modes.

Assessment:

> Conceptually right, literally overstated. Treat as a threat profile, not a guarantee.
