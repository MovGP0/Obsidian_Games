# Immortality

Immortality is an expansion for base Dune: Imperium, centered on Bene Tleilax research and genetic manipulation.

## What It Adds

- Research track.
- Tleilaxu track.
- Specimens.
- Graft cards.
- Replacement starting cards.
- New intrigue and Imperium cards.

## Strategic Shift

Immortality makes deck manipulation and research timing more important. The research track gives immediate bonuses and unlocks genetic markers that improve future card effects.

## Practical Notes

- Research is strongest when it supports a coherent deck plan.
- Graft rewards hand management and card pairing.
- Specimens are a conversion resource, not a goal.
- The expansion can make trashing and card draw more valuable than in base-only games.
