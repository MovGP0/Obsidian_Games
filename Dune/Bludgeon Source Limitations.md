# Bludgeon Source Limitations

The Bludgeon material here comes from accessible indexed excerpts and snippets, not a full timestamped transcript dump.

Treat the comparisons as high-confidence where quoted, but not as a full line-by-line transcript audit of the whole classic-Dune catalogue.

These notes are intentionally anchored in a **standard six-player basic-game baseline**. If your group usually plays advanced combat, double spice blow, or tourney-style clarifications, the relative value of Fremen, Emperor, Karama timing, and certain Bene Gesserit and Guild tricks changes materially.
