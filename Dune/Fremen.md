# Fremen

The Fremen faction track is one of the four main influence tracks in Dune: Imperium.

## Strategic Identity

Fremen access often connects to [[Dune/Water|water]], desert spaces, troop deployment, and spice access. It can be a strong route when water unlocks the next part of your economy.

## When To Prioritize

Prioritize Fremen when:

- You need water or desert access.
- Your deck has Fremen icons or Fremen payoff cards.
- You can convert the influence point into a broader scoring route.
- You are playing [[Dune/Uprising|Uprising]] lines where desert and worm pressure matter.
