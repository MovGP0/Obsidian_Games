# Fremen

You win through planet control and endurance.

Fremen often look weaker to beginners because they are not as dominant in bidding or shipping as Emperor or Guild. That is misleading in long games.

In the basic game, Fremen's problem is not movement. It is **equipment timing**. You are poor, can rarely buy cards early, and should wait for accessible spice blows while avoiding premature battles.

Use your deployment freedom and 2-space movement to occupy neglected geometry, not to launch fair early fights. Midgame, your best rounds are the rounds after other factions have already spent spice and cards hurting each other.

Late game, keep the special victory condition in mind earlier than most players do. The table often remembers it one turn too late.

Advanced rules materially improve Fremen's combat economics. In pure basic, be more restrained.

Basic plan:

- Use native movement and deployment advantages.
- Build quietly.
- Preserve forces.
- Avoid fair early fights.
- Become harder to remove over time.
- Keep alternative win conditions in mind if your ruleset uses them.
