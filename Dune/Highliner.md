# Highliner

Highliner is a major combat lever because it can turn stored [[Dune/Spice|spice]] into a large troop commitment.

## Strategic Role

Highliner matters most in the middle-to-late game, especially when Conflict III rewards are live. Six spice is often a tactical threshold: a player holding it may threaten a point conflict even if their board position looks quiet.

## What To Watch

- Who has enough spice?
- Who has enough troops to make the shipment matter?
- Is the current conflict worth the spend?
- Can another player beat the combat total after reveal and intrigue?
