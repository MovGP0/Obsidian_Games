# Emperor

The Emperor faction track is one of the four main influence tracks in Dune: Imperium.

## Strategic Identity

Emperor access often connects to [[Dune/Solari|solari]], troop support, and political tempo. It can help fund [[Dune/Swordmaster|Swordmaster]], [[Dune/High Council|High Council]], or combat plans depending on the board.

## When To Prioritize

Prioritize Emperor when:

- Your cards provide reliable Emperor access.
- You need solari to unlock a tempo upgrade.
- The alliance race is realistic.
- The faction point fits your next scoring plan.

Do not chase Emperor influence only because solari feels useful. The track still needs to become points or denial.
