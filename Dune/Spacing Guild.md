# Spacing Guild

The Spacing Guild faction track is one of the four main influence tracks in Dune: Imperium.

## Strategic Identity

Spacing Guild access often connects to [[Dune/Spice|spice]], shipping-style tempo, and expensive conversion turns. It is especially important when the game revolves around [[Dune/Highliner|Highliner]], [[Dune/The Spice Must Flow|The Spice Must Flow]], or [[Dune/Rise of Ix|Ix]] economy.

## When To Prioritize

Prioritize Spacing Guild when:

- You need spice or shipping pressure.
- You can contest the alliance.
- Your deck has Guild icons or Guild payoff cards.
- The faction point supports your late-game route.
