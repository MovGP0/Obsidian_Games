# Rise of Ix

Rise of Ix is an expansion for base Dune: Imperium.

## What It Adds

- Interstellar Shipping and the freighter track.
- Tech tiles.
- Dreadnoughts.
- CHOAM board changes.
- New leaders, cards, intrigue, and conflicts.

## Strategic Shift

Shipping becomes a major economy and tempo lane. Bludgeon transcript summaries for Ix openings emphasize racing to useful solari and shipping positions, especially when trying to reach Swordmaster quickly.

## Practical Notes

- Interstellar Shipping can become a central contested space.
- Dreadnoughts make conflict commitment more durable.
- Tech tiles create build-around incentives.
- The expansion rewards planning your late-game point route earlier than the base game.

## Watch For

Do not chase shipping only because it is strong. It must convert into points, cards, tech, combat leverage, or faction pressure.
