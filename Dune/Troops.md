# Troops

Troops are the main resource spent in [[Dune/Combat and Conflicts|conflicts]].

## Strategic Role

Troops are not valuable because they exist in your garrison. They are valuable because they can win or threaten rewards.

Spend troops when:

- The conflict reward is worth the commitment.
- You can reach first place or a valuable lower reward.
- The timing supports your [[Dune/Game Phase Identification|phase]].
- You are not weakening yourself before a better conflict.
