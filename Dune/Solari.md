# Solari

Solari is the money resource. Its main strategic job is buying tempo and premium board access.

## Main Uses

- Buy [[Dune/Swordmaster|Swordmaster]].
- Buy [[Dune/High Council|High Council]].
- Use Mentat or similar extra-agent effects.
- Pay for specific board or card effects.

## Strategic Role

Early solari usually matters because it creates another agent or strengthens reveal turns. Later solari matters only if it still converts into points, denial, or high-value actions.

Do not hoard solari unless you know the conversion. It is a resource, not a score.
